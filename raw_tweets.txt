The J-space contains individual words that are related to the words and phrases that the model is most likely to spit out in a response in the near future. If Claude were a person (which it is not), you might say that these hidden words can reveal what’s on its mind before it actually speaks. Anthropic found that what an LLM is actually doing can often be different from what it says it is doing. The company claims that monitoring words that pop up in the J-space gives it a new way to understand and control its models. The company shared its results in a paper posted on its website this week.
